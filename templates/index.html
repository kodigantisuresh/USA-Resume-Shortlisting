Project Overview: AI-Based Resume Shortlisting System

This project presents an AI-driven Resume Shortlisting System aimed at optimizing and automating the initial stages of the recruitment process. By leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML) techniques, the system is capable of efficiently parsing and analyzing resumes to identify qualified candidates. It performs automated screening based on predefined criteria, matches candidate skills with job-specific requirements, and evaluates relevant experienceâ€”particularly in government sectors. The system then ranks candidates using a composite scoring mechanism, thereby enabling recruiters to make informed decisions with greater speed and accuracy. The overall objective is to enhance the efficiency, consistency, and objectivity of candidate shortlisting in modern hiring workflows.

Key Features

Automated Screening

The system efficiently filters resumes by applying predefined criteria such as job title relevance, required qualifications, and minimum experience. This reduces manual effort and ensures only eligible candidates proceed to the next stage.

Skill Matching

Utilizing NLP techniques, the system extracts and analyzes skills from candidate resumes and compares them against the required job specifications. This facilitates precise identification of candidates whose competencies align with organizational needs.

Ranking System

Candidates are ranked based on a composite relevance score derived from two primary factors: the percentage of matched skills, Experience and the experience in government . This ensures the most suitable candidates are prioritized in the selection process.

User-Friendly Interface

The solution offers an intuitive and accessible interface that enables automated resume screening directly from email submissions, significantly reducing manual effort for recruiters. It allows users to configure screening parameters and seamlessly review ranked candidate results, thereby streamlining the recruitment workflow and enhancing overall efficiency.
